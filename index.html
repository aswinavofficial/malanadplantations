Puttady, a small plantation town located in the high ranges of Idukki, is home to the Malanad Passion Fruit Plantations. The place, blessed with its salubrious climate grows the native Brazilian tropical fruit, Passiflora edulis, commonly known as Passion fruit.

Pleasantly sweet and tart, passion fruit is a brimming source of numerous antioxidants, minerals, vitamins, and fibers. Moreover the sustainable organic production practices followed in the farm ensures our customers the best farm-to-fork services.
